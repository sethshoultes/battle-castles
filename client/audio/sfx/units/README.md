# Unit Sound Effects

This directory contains unit-specific sound effects for deployment, movement, and death.

## Expected Files

### Knight Sounds
- `knight_deploy.ogg` - Knight deployment sound
- `knight_move.ogg` - Knight movement/footsteps (armor clanking)
- `knight_attack_grunt.ogg` - Knight attack vocalization
- `knight_hurt.ogg` - Knight taking damage
- `knight_death.ogg` - Knight death sound

### Archer Sounds
- `archer_deploy.ogg` - Archer deployment sound
- `archer_move.ogg` - Archer movement (lighter footsteps)
- `archer_draw.ogg` - Archer drawing bow
- `archer_hurt.ogg` - Archer taking damage
- `archer_death.ogg` - Archer death sound

### Wizard Sounds
- `wizard_deploy.ogg` - Wizard deployment sound
- `wizard_move.ogg` - Wizard movement (robes, staff)
- `wizard_chant.ogg` - Wizard casting chant
- `wizard_hurt.ogg` - Wizard taking damage
- `wizard_death.ogg` - Wizard death sound

### Giant Sounds
- `giant_deploy.ogg` - Giant deployment (heavy thud)
- `giant_move.ogg` - Giant footsteps (heavy, earth-shaking)
- `giant_roar.ogg` - Giant battle roar
- `giant_hurt.ogg` - Giant taking damage
- `giant_death.ogg` - Giant death (heavy fall)

### Dragon Sounds
- `dragon_deploy.ogg` - Dragon deployment (wing flaps, roar)
- `dragon_fly.ogg` - Dragon flying (wing flaps loop)
- `dragon_roar.ogg` - Dragon roar
- `dragon_breathe_fire.ogg` - Dragon breathing fire
- `dragon_hurt.ogg` - Dragon taking damage
- `dragon_death.ogg` - Dragon death

### Catapult Sounds
- `catapult_deploy.ogg` - Catapult deployment (wood creaking)
- `catapult_reload.ogg` - Catapult reloading
- `catapult_fire.ogg` - Catapult firing
- `catapult_destroyed.ogg` - Catapult being destroyed

### Healer Sounds
- `healer_deploy.ogg` - Healer deployment
- `healer_move.ogg` - Healer movement
- `healer_cast.ogg` - Healer casting heal
- `healer_hurt.ogg` - Healer taking damage
- `healer_death.ogg` - Healer death

### Generic/Shared
- `unit_spawn.ogg` - Generic spawn effect
- `unit_despawn.ogg` - Generic despawn effect
- `footstep_grass.ogg` - Footstep on grass
- `footstep_stone.ogg` - Footstep on stone
- `footstep_dirt.ogg` - Footstep on dirt

## Voice Lines (Optional)
- `knight_battlecry.ogg` - "For the kingdom!"
- `archer_ready.ogg` - "Arrows ready!"
- `wizard_spell_ready.ogg` - "Magic at your command!"
- `giant_angry.ogg` - Giant angry growl

## File Format
- Format: OGG Vorbis (.ogg) or MP3 (.mp3)
- Duration: 0.5-3 seconds for most sounds
- Sample rate: 44.1 kHz or 48 kHz
