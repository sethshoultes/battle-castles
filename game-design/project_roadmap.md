# Project Roadmap & Production Timeline

**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** October 30, 2025  
**Document Type:** Project Planning & Schedule

---

## Executive Summary

This document outlines the complete development roadmap for Battle Castles, from initial concept to global launch and beyond. The project is structured in phases with clear milestones, deliverables, and resource allocation.

**Total Development Time:** 9 months (pre-production to launch)  
**Team Size:** 15-20 people  
**Estimated Budget:** $1.2M - $1.8M

---

## Team Structure

**Functionality:**

- No crash bugs

- Economy progression feels fair

- < 100ms average latency

- **Week 24:** Release Candidate (RC) ready

### Success Criteria
✅ Zero P0 bugs remaining  
✅ P1 bugs < 10  
✅ Player satisfaction score > 4/5  
✅ Retention D1 > 40%, D7 > 20%

## Phase 5: Soft Launch (Month 8)

**Duration:** 4 weeks  
**Team Size:** Full team + support staff

### Objectives
- Validate game in real market
- Test monetization
- Collect large-scale data
- Iterate based on feedback

### Launch Markets (Soft Launch)
1. **Canada** - English-speaking, similar to US market
2. **Philippines** - Android-heavy, price-sensitive
3. **Australia** - Good test for global English audience

### Deliverables

**Week 25-26 (Launch Prep):**
- [ ] App Store / Google Play listings created
- [ ] Privacy policy and terms of service finalized
- [ ] Payment systems integrated and tested
- [ ] Customer support system ready (help desk)
- [ ] Social media accounts created
- [ ] Community management guidelines

**Week 27-28 (Soft Launch Live):**
- [ ] Released to test markets
- [ ] Daily KPI monitoring
- [ ] Hot fixes deployed as needed
- [ ] A/B testing monetization
- [ ] Player feedback collected
- [ ] Balance patches if needed

### Key Metrics to Monitor

**Engagement:**
- DAU / MAU
- Average session length: Target 15+ minutes
- Battles per day: Target 8-12
- Retention: D1 > 40%, D7 > 20%, D30 > 8%

**Monetization:**
- Conversion rate: Target 5-8%
- ARPU: Target $1.50-2.50
- Average Battle Pass attach: Target 15%
- IAP success rate: Target > 90%

**Technical:**
- Crash rate: < 1%
- API errors: < 0.5%
- Average matchmaking time: < 30 seconds
- Server uptime: > 99.5%

### Success Criteria
✅ Retention meets targets (D7 > 20%)  
✅ Monetization validated (ARPU > $1)  
✅ No critical technical issues  
✅ Player sentiment positive (4+ star rating)

## Risk Management

### Critical Risks & Mitigation

**Risk 1: Scope Creep**
- **Mitigation:** Strict feature lock after Month 6
- **Backup Plan:** Cut non-essential features to meet launch date

**Risk 2: Team Attrition**
- **Mitigation:** Competitive compensation, good work culture
- **Backup Plan:** Cross-train team members, maintain documentation

**Risk 3: Poor Soft Launch Performance**
- **Mitigation:** Rapid iteration based on data
- **Backup Plan:** Delay global launch, fix core issues

**Risk 4: Technical Issues at Launch**
- **Mitigation:** Over-provision servers, comprehensive testing
- **Backup Plan:** Emergency rollback procedure, 24/7 on-call team

**Risk 5: Competitive Market**
- **Mitigation:** Unique mechanics, superior polish
- **Backup Plan:** Pivot marketing, find niche audience
